Key Features

Our platform offers a comprehensive set of features designed to streamline the entire process.

Inventory Management

Retailers can easily track stock levels, set expiration dates, and manage product listings from a centralized dashboard.

Dynamic Pricing

Set discount rates based on expiration dates, with automatic price adjustments as products near their expiry date.

Advanced Search

Customers can search and filter products by category, price range, expiration date, and discount percentage.

Secure Checkout

Safe and streamlined payment processing with multiple payment options and encrypted transaction handling.

Route Optimization

Delivery agents benefit from intelligent route planning that minimizes travel time and maximizes delivery efficiency.

Real-time Notifications

All users receive timely updates about order status, delivery progress, and special promotions.
