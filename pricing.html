Pricing FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our pricing plans.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes to your subscription will take effect immediately. If you upgrade mid-billing cycle, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, the changes will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

We offer a 20% discount on all paid plans when you choose annual billing instead of monthly. We also have special discounts for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information about special pricing options.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. We also support payments through PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For enterprise customers, we can arrange direct bank transfers or invoicing as needed.

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all paid plans. This gives you full access to all features so you can determine if the plan meets your needs. No credit card is required to start a trial. At the end of your trial, you can choose to subscribe or downgrade to a free plan.

You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the free plan. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you're welcome to use the service until your paid period ends.

Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the final sale price of each item sold through our platform. For example, if you sell an item for ₹100 on the Basic plan with an 18% commission rate, we would take ₹180, and you would receive $8.20. Commission rates vary by plan: Basic (18%), Professional (15%), and Enterprise (12%).

On the Basic plan, you can list up to 50 items per month. If you reach this limit, you won't be able to add new items until the next billing cycle begins. You can still manage and sell your existing listings. If you regularly need to list more than 50 items, we recommend upgrading to the Professional plan, which offers unlimited listings.

Advanced inventory management includes features like batch operations, automated inventory adjustments based on expiration dates, category management, inventory forecasting, and detailed inventory reports. It also includes tools to help you track waste reduction metrics and optimize your pricing strategy based on historical sales data.

Enhanced listing visibility gives your products priority placement in search results and browsing pages. Professional plan listings appear higher than Basic plan listings, while Enterprise plan listings receive top priority. This increased visibility typically results in 30-50% more views and higher sales conversion rates compared to standard visibility.

No, there are no long-term contracts required. All plans operate on a month-to-month basis, or you can choose annual billing for a 20% discount. You can cancel at any time without penalty. For Enterprise customers with custom requirements, we may offer special contract terms, but these are always optional.

ClearX+ typically pays for itself if you place at least 2-3 orders per month. With free delivery (normally ₹399-₹599 per order), you can recoup the ₹999 monthly fee with just 2 orders. Additionally, you'll benefit from 2x reward points, early access to deals, and exclusive offers, which provide even more value. We recommend trying our 14-day free trial to see if it works for your shopping habits.

You earn 10 points for every ₹85 spent on ClearX. Basic members earn at the standard 1x rate, while ClearX+ members earn at 2x (20 points per ₹85). Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders at a rate of 1,000 points = ₹500 off. Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. You can check your points balance and available rewards in your account dashboard.

ClearX+ members receive exclusive offers not available to Basic members. These include special discounts from premium retailers, flash sales with deeper discounts, seasonal promotions, and occasional free items with qualifying purchases. We typically release 3-5 exclusive offers each week, which are highlighted in the "Member Exclusives" section of the app.

Yes, your ClearX+ membership can be shared with up to 4 additional family members living at the same address. To add family members, go to your account settings and select "Manage Family Members." Each person will need to create their own ClearX account and verify their address. All orders placed by family members will earn 2x points and enjoy free delivery.

ClearX+ members receive notifications and access to new deals 30 minutes before they become available to Basic members. This is particularly valuable for limited-quantity items from popular retailers, which often sell out quickly. You'll receive a push notification when early access begins, allowing you to browse and purchase these items before they're available to everyone else.

Earnings vary based on your location, hours worked, and delivery volume. Standard plan agents typically earn ₹15-20 per hour, while Premium plan agents average ₹18-25 per hour due to higher commission rates and priority access to high-value orders. In busy urban areas, earnings can be higher. You'll also receive 100% of customer tips in addition to your delivery earnings.

Basic route optimization provides simple point-to-point directions between pickup and delivery locations. Advanced route optimization uses AI to analyze traffic patterns, delivery windows, and order grouping to create the most efficient routes. It can handle multiple pickups and deliveries in a single trip, helping Premium agents complete more deliveries per hour and maximize earnings while minimizing travel time and fuel costs.

The accident insurance included with the Premium plan covers you while you're actively delivering orders on the ClearX platform. It provides up to ₹1 million in liability coverage, as well as coverage for medical expenses resulting from accidents during deliveries. It also includes coverage for damaged goods. This insurance is supplemental to your personal auto insurance and helps protect you financially while working as a delivery agent.

Premium plan members receive minimum guaranteed pay during scheduled delivery blocks. If you earn less than the guaranteed minimum (typically ₹18 per hour in most markets) during your scheduled hours due to low order volume, we'll make up the difference. To qualify, you must be available and accept at least 85% of offered deliveries during your scheduled block.

The Premium plan is typically worth it if you deliver at least 15-20 hours per week. The higher commission rate (85% vs 75%) means you earn approximately ₹2-3 more per hour, which quickly covers the ₹19.99 monthly fee. Additionally, priority access to high-value orders, advanced route optimization, and accident insurance provide significant value. We recommend starting with the Standard plan and upgrading once you're consistently delivering 15+ hours weekly.
